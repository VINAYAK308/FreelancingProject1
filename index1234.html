Ministry Of Steel

Backbone of Indian Infra

Co-ordination and planning of the growth and development of Iron and Steel Industry in the country ( including Re-rolling Mills, Alloy Steel and Ferro Alloy Industries, Refractories) both in the Public and Private Sectors;

Formulation of policies in respect of production, pricing, distribution, import and export of iron & steel, ferro alloys and refractories;

Development of input industries relating to iron ore, manganese ore, chrome ore and Refractories etc. , required mainly by the steel industry.

Green Steel Initiative

The Green Steel Initiative by the Ministry of Steel is a comprehensive plan aimed at decarbonizing the Indian steel industry and reducing its environmental footprint launched in December 2024.

PLI Scheme

PLI Scheme Launching

Launched on July 29, 2021 the scheme focuses to boost domestic production of specialty steel grades, which are high-value and have applications in various sectors like defense, automobiles, and electrical appliances. This reduces reliance on imports and promotes "Atmanirbhar Bharat" (self-reliant India).

Modernization of Steel Plants

The Ministry is supporting and encouraging pilot projects to demonstrate the feasibility and effectiveness of CCUS technologies in Indian steel plants. This includes initiatives like:
Tata Steel's 5 TPD CO2 capture plant at Jamshedpur.
JSW's 100 TPD CO2 capture unit at their DRI plant in Salav.
JSPL's 3000 TPD capacity Carbon Capturing Unit at Angul, Odisha.
